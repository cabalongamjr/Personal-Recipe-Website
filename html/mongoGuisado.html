Authentic Filipino Mongo Guisado: A Hearty and Flavorful Dish!

Experience the heartwarming flavors of Filipino cuisine with our Mongo Guisado recipe! This hearty stew features tender mung beans simmered with savory pork, flavorful vegetables, and aromatic spices. Comforting and satisfying, it's a perfect dish for family gatherings or everyday meals. Try it and delight your taste buds!

Servings: 4-6

Preparation Time: 15 minutes (plus soaking time)

Cooking Time: 1-1.5 hours

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mung beans (monggo), soaked for at least 1 hour
  • 1/2 lb pork belly or pork shoulder, cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 medium-sized potato, diced
  • 1 cup spinach or malunggay leaves
  • 4 cups water or broth
  • Fish sauce (patis), to taste
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Cooking oil

Instructions

  1. In a pot, heat cooking oil over medium heat. Saut√© onions and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add pork cubes and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Stir in chopped tomatoes and cook until softened.
  4. Add soaked mung beans and water or broth. Bring to a boil then simmer until mung beans are tender, about 40-50 minutes.
  5. Add diced potatoes and continue to simmer until potatoes are cooked through.
  6. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper according to taste.
  7. Finally, add spinach or malunggay leaves and cook until wilted.
  8. Remove from heat and serve hot. Enjoy your delicious Mongo Guisado!
