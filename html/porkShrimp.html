Authentic Pork and Shrimp Pancit: A Flavorful Stir-Fry Delight!

Indulge in the delightful flavors of Filipino cuisine with our Pork and Shrimp Pancit recipe! This classic stir-fried noodle dish features tender pork, succulent shrimp, crisp vegetables, and flavorful seasonings. Quick and easy to make, it's perfect for busy weeknight dinners or special occasions. Try it and satisfy your cravings!

Servings: 4-6

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 20-25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb pork belly or pork shoulder, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 8 oz pancit canton noodles (or any egg noodles)
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp fish sauce (patis)
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • Cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Heat cooking oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Saut√© garlic until fragrant, then add onions and cook until translucent.
  3. Add pork slices and cook until browned and cooked through.
  4. Push pork to one side of the pan and add shrimp. Cook until pink and opaque, then mix with the pork.
  5. Pour chicken broth into the pan and bring to a simmer.
  6. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, and ground black pepper. Stir to combine.
  7. Place pancit canton noodles into the pan and gently toss with the sauce until noodles are fully coated and softened.
  8. Add julienned carrots and sliced bell peppers. Cook for an additional 3-4 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  9. Remove from heat and transfer to a serving platter. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Pork and Shrimp Pancit!
