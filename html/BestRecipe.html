Authentic Filipino Lechon: A Flavorful Delight!

Experience the joy of Filipino cuisine with our Authentic Lechon recipe! Succulent pork leg, roasted to crispy perfection. Easy steps, unforgettable taste. Perfect for any occasion. Try it today and savor the flavor of tradition!

Servings: 10-12

Preparation Time: 4 hours (marinating) + 30 minutes

Cooking Time: 4-5 hours

Ingredients

  • 1 whole pork leg (approximately 5-6 kg)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 10 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp ground black pepper
  • 5 bay leaves
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, onion, salt, and pepper to create the marinade.
  3. Rub the marinade all over the pork leg, making sure to get into all the crevices. Let it marinate for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight.
  4. Place the marinated pork leg on a roasting rack in a large roasting pan. Add bay leaves on top.
  5. Brush the pork leg with vegetable oil to ensure crispy skin.
  6. Roast in the preheated oven for 4 to 5 hours, or until the skin is crispy and the meat is tender.
  7. Allow the Lechon to rest for 20 minutes before carving. Serve with your favorite dipping sauce.
