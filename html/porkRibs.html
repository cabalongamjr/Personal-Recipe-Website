Authentic Filipino Lechon-style Pork Ribs: A Flavorful Delight!

Experience the rich flavors of Filipino cuisine with our Lechon-style Pork Ribs recipe! Tender pork ribs, roasted to perfection with a crispy exterior. Simple steps, extraordinary taste. Perfect for any gathering. Give it a try and enjoy a taste of tradition!

Servings: 6-8

Preparation Time: 4 hours (marinating) + 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 1.5-2 hours

Ingredients

  • 2 racks of pork ribs (about 2-3 kg total)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 8 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp ground black pepper
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, onion, salt, and pepper to create the marinade.
  3. Rub the marinade all over the pork ribs, ensuring even coverage. Let them marinate for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight.
  4. Place the marinated ribs on a roasting rack in a large roasting pan. Add bay leaves on top.
  5. Brush the ribs with vegetable oil to ensure a crispy finish.
  6. Roast in the preheated oven for 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the ribs are tender and the exterior is crispy.
  7. Allow the ribs to rest for 10 minutes before slicing. Serve with your favorite dipping sauce.
