Authentic Filipino Pancit Fried Noodles: A Flavorful Stir-Fry Delight!

Experience the delightful flavors of Filipino cuisine with our Pancit Fried Noodles recipe! This classic stir-fry dish features thin rice noodles stir-fried with savory meats, crisp vegetables, and aromatic seasonings. Quick and easy to make, it's perfect for weeknight dinners or special occasions. Try it and enjoy a taste of the Philippines!

Servings: 4-6

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 20-25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 8 oz pancit bihon (rice noodles)
  • 1/2 lb chicken breast or pork, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp fish sauce (patis)
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • Cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Soak pancit bihon noodles in warm water for about 10-15 minutes or until softened. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat cooking oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Saut√© garlic until fragrant, then add onions and cook until translucent.
  4. Add chicken or pork slices and cook until browned and cooked through.
  5. Push meat to one side of the pan and add shrimp. Cook until pink and opaque, then mix with the meat.
  6. Pour chicken broth into the pan and bring to a simmer.
  7. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, and ground black pepper. Stir to combine.
  8. Place softened pancit bihon noodles into the pan and gently toss with the sauce until fully coated and softened.
  9. Add julienned carrots and sliced bell peppers. Cook for an additional 3-4 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  10. Remove from heat and transfer to a serving platter. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Pancit Fried Noodles!
