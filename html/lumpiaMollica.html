Authentic Filipino Lumpia Mollica: A Crispy and Flavorful Delight!

Indulge in the delicious flavors of Filipino cuisine with our Lumpia Mollica recipe! These crispy and savory spring rolls are filled with a tasty mixture of ground pork, vegetables, and spices. Easy to make and perfect for snacking or as an appetizer. Try it and delight your taste buds!

Servings: 12-15 pieces

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Cooking Time: 20-25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 1 medium carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup cabbage, finely shredded
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • Lumpia wrappers
  • Cooking oil, for frying

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine ground pork, carrot, cabbage, onion, garlic, soy sauce, salt, and black pepper. Mix well.
  2. Place a lumpia wrapper on a clean surface. Spoon about 2 tablespoons of the pork mixture onto the bottom third of the wrapper.
  3. Fold the bottom edge of the wrapper over the filling, then fold in the sides, and roll tightly into a log shape.
  4. Brush the top edge of the wrapper with beaten egg to seal the lumpia.
  5. Heat cooking oil in a deep pan over medium heat. Fry the lumpia in batches until golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  6. Remove the cooked lumpia from the oil and drain on paper towels. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce. Enjoy!
