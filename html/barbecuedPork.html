Authentic Barbecued Pork Kebabs: A Flavorful Grilling Adventure!

Embark on a delicious grilling adventure with our Barbecued Pork Kebabs recipe! These succulent skewers feature marinated pork chunks, vibrant vegetables, and a tantalizing barbecue glaze. Perfect for summer cookouts or any occasion. Fire up the grill and savor the flavors of barbecue bliss!

Servings: 4-6

Preparation Time: 30 minutes (plus marinating time)

Cooking Time: 10-15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pork shoulder, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 red onion, cut into chunks
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1/4 cup barbecue sauce
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Wooden or metal skewers

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine barbecue sauce, soy sauce, minced garlic, brown sugar, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to create the marinade.
  2. Add pork cubes to the marinade and toss to coat evenly. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for best results.
  3. Preheat grill to medium-high heat. If using wooden skewers, soak them in water for 30 minutes to prevent burning.
  4. Thread marinated pork cubes, bell pepper chunks, red onion chunks, and zucchini slices onto skewers, alternating between ingredients.
  5. Brush the assembled kebabs with any remaining marinade.
  6. Grill kebabs over direct heat for 10-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until pork is cooked through and vegetables are tender and slightly charred.
  7. Remove kebabs from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy your flavorful Barbecued Pork Kebabs!
